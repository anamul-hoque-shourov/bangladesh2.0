Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 embraces advanced technologies, fostering research, startups, and digital transformation to enhance productivity and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Prioritizing renewable energy, eco-friendly practices, and resource efficiency, Bangladesh 2.0 commits to a balanced and resilient environmental future.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 develops smart infrastructure, integrating modern transport, energy grids, and urban planning to support sustainable economic growth and connectivity.

Empowerment

By promoting education, skills training, and inclusive policies, Bangladesh 2.0 ensures equitable opportunities, uplifting communities and fostering individual potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 is more than a vision; it is a transformative journey toward a brighter, sustainable, and inclusive future. Through innovation, we are redefining possibilities; through sustainability, we are protecting our resources for generations to come; through robust infrastructure, we are building a resilient foundation for growth; and through empowerment, we are unlocking the potential of every citizen. Together, we are shaping a nation where dreams are nurtured, opportunities are boundless, and progress is shared by all. This is our commitment to a prosperous Bangladesh—a Bangladesh 2.0."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
